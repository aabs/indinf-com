While I was out trawling the web, I came upon extisp.ico.us – an extremely cool little CGI app to render a tag cloud of a user's public links in del.icio.us.

Check it out on a friend's collection – it gives you an insight into their mind. As Aymeric, who sits beside me, said when he saw his links: 'zis is like looking at my life!'
